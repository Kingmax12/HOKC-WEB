About HOKC

God's Happy Family - Heirs of the Kingdom Chapel

Our Story

A Journey of Faith

Heirs of the Kingdom Chapel (HOKC) is a vibrant Christian community located at Babcock University, Ilishan-Remo, Ogun State. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

Our church believes in the power of fellowship, the importance of biblical teaching, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, building lasting relationships, and making a positive impact in our community and beyond.

At HOKC, we embrace the motto "God's Happy Family" because we believe that the church should be a place of joy, acceptance, and belonging. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or community resident, you'll find a warm welcome here.

What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

The Bible: We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and practice.

The Trinity: We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ: We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and triumphant return.

Salvation: We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Church: We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission.

Eternal Life: We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost—the saved to eternal life and the lost to eternal separation from God.
