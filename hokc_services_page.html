Service Times

Join Us in Worship

At HOKC, we gather together to worship God, grow in our faith, and build meaningful relationships. All are welcome to join us at any of our services. Come as you are and experience God's presence with us!

🌅

Sabbath Worship

8:45 AM

📍 Buth 600 Seater Auditorium

Start your Saturday morning with powerful worship, inspiring messages from God's Word, and fellowship with believers. This is our main weekly gathering where the whole church family comes together.

📖

Bible Study

8:30 PM

📍 Buth 600 Seater Auditorium

Dive deeper into Scripture in our evening Bible study. Through careful examination of God's Word, we grow in knowledge and understanding together as we apply biblical truths to our daily lives.

Evening Service

6:00 PM

📍 Buth 600 Seater Auditorium

End your day with worship and prayer. Our evening service provides a peaceful atmosphere to reflect, worship, and receive encouragement for the week ahead.

Wednesday Worship
